About Me

I'm Jigah Favour Sokoato, a passionate Front-End Developer with an engineering background and extensive experience in teamwork. An alumnus of the prestigious Federal University of Technology Minna, I excel in crafting visually stunning and user-centric web experiences.

With proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I bring code to life, ensuring seamless interactions and captivating interfaces. My expertise extends to working with frameworks like Tailwind CSS, allowing me to deliver modern and efficient web solutions.

Beyond the world of web development, I find joy in exploring my passion for video editing in my free time. The art of storytelling through visuals has taught me to look at web development with an artistic lens, infusing every project with a touch of creativity and flair.

Let's collaborate to create impactful digital solutions that elevate brands and leave a lasting impression. Welcome to my world of web craftsmanship and collaborative excellence!

What can I do for you?

Responsive & Interactive Webpage

I create fully responsive and interactive webpages using the latest web technologies like HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. Your website will adapt seamlessly to various devices and screen sizes, with smooth interactions and engaging user experiences.

Mobile App Development

I develop mobile applications for Android and iOS platforms using native technologies and frameworks like React Native. Your app will have excellent performance and user-friendly interfaces, delivering a seamless user experience.

Video Editing

Apart from web development, I have a passion for video editing. With attention to detail and creativity, I edit and produce visually appealing videos that tell compelling stories for your promotional content, vlogs, or cinematic projects.
